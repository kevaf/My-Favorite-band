About.

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada, consisting of lead singer Dan Reynolds, lead guitarist Wayne Sermon, bassist Ben McKee, and drummer Daniel Platzman. Identifying as 'genreless', the imagine dragons was formed in 2009. They first unveiled their strong passion and gernre-bending abilities with a series independently released albums that quickly earned grassroot following. According to one of the band members, the name Imagine Dragons, is an anagram made from letters of different words.

Band Members

Dan Coulter Reynolds (born July 14, 1987) is a singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is the lead vocalist of the pop rock band Imagine Dragons. He is originally the founder and the first member of the boy band group The Imagine Dragons.

Daniel James Platzman (born September 28, 1986) is a songwriter, record producer and composer. He is the drummer for the band.

Daniel Wayne Sermon (born June 15, 1984) is a songwriter, and record producer. Daniel is the lead guitarist for the band.

Benjamin Arthur McKee (born April 7, 1985) is a songwriter and record producer. He is the band's bassist.
